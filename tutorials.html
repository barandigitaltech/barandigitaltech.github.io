Natural Everyday Look

Follow this beginner-friendly guide with step-by-step instructions and visuals.

  1. Apply a lightweight foundation evenly.
  2. Use a blending sponge to smooth out coverage.
  3. Add a soft blush on the cheeks.
  4. Apply neutral eyeshadow tones for subtle effect.
  5. Finish with mascara and tinted lip balm.
