Top Picks for Beginners

  • Hydrating Foundation – Lightweight formula ideal for dry or sensitive skin
  • Lengthening Mascara – Easy to apply and remove, perfect for newcomers
  • Neutral Eyeshadow Palette – Shades suitable for everyday looks
  • Blending Sponge – Helps achieve a natural finish with foundation or concealer
  • Brow Gel – Quick styling for neat, natural brows

Tips for Choosing the Right Products

  • Stick with brands that offer beginner-friendly tutorials
  • Look for products labeled hypoallergenic if you have sensitive skin
  • Try travel-size versions before committing to full size
