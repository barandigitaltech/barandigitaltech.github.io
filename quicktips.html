Tips to Make Your Makeup Last

  • Start with a clean canvas: Wash and moisturize your face before applying any makeup.
  • Use a primer: Apply a silicone-based or hydrating primer to smooth skin and extend wear time.
  • Layer strategically: Use thin layers of foundation and concealer to avoid caking.
  • Set your base: Lightly dust translucent powder on oily areas like the T-zone.
  • Lock it in: Finish with a setting spray to seal your look and add a natural finish.
  • Blot, don’t wipe: Use blotting paper to absorb excess oil without disturbing makeup.
  • Choose long-wear formulas: Opt for waterproof mascara and smudge-proof eyeliner for all-day wear.
  • Touch-up smart: Keep a compact kit with concealer, powder, and lip balm for quick fixes.

Bonus Pro Tips for a Flawless Look

  • Apply in natural light: It helps you see true colors and blend more evenly.
  • Use multi-tasking products: Cream blushes can double as lip tints for a cohesive look.
  • Don’t skip skincare: Hydrated skin holds makeup better and looks more radiant.
  • Clean your tools: Dirty brushes can cause patchy application and breakouts.
  • Practice makes perfect: The more you experiment, the more confident you'll become!
